1.  Which agent architecture is characterized by direct stimulus-response rules, with no planning or consideration of past experiences?
    a) Hybrid Architecture
    b) Deliberative Architecture
    c) Reactive Architecture
    d) Learning Architecture

2.  What is the primary advantage of a **Deliberative Architecture**?
    a) Fast response times.
    b) Simple to implement.
    c) Ability to plan and reason about future actions.
    d) Low computational cost.

3.  Which architecture combines the strengths of both reactive and deliberative approaches?
    a) Reactive Architecture
    b) Deliberative Architecture
    c) Hybrid Architecture
    d) Rule-Based Architecture

---
**Answer Key**

1.  c)
2.  c)
3.  c)
