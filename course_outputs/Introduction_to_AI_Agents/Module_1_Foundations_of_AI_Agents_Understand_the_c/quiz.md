1.  Which of the following BEST describes an AI Agent?
    a) A human programmer who interacts with a system.
    b) An autonomous entity that perceives its environment through sensors and acts upon that environment using actuators to achieve specific goals.
    c) A software program that only receives data.
    d) A database that stores information.

2.  A robot vacuum cleaner operating in a home is an example of which type of environment?
    a) Fully Observable, Deterministic, Static
    b) Partially Observable, Stochastic, Dynamic
    c) Fully Observable, Stochastic, Dynamic
    d) Partially Observable, Deterministic, Static

3.  In the context of AI Agents, what does the term "Rationality" refer to?
    a) An agent's ability to perfectly solve any problem.
    b) An agent's ability to operate without any external influence.
    c) An agent's ability to choose actions that are expected to maximize its goal achievement, given its current state and knowledge.
    d) An agent's ability to learn from its mistakes.

---
**Answer Key**

1.  b)
2.  b)
3.  c)
